IMDb ratings visualizations

To use this website you will need to export your ratings from your IMDb account:

Login to your IMDb account.

Click on your name.

Click on "Your ratings".

In the "Your ratings" page, click on the "Export" button and follow the instructions.

You can now upload your CSV using the input below.
